This is our attempt at owning our own corner on the internet. The corner where we can plaster things up to share with you. We’re not quite sure where it’s going, but we’ll all see along the way how it changes and evolves. Ultimately, we just want to have some fun and hope you don’ get too bored. 
At the start of each month, we’ll update you with 12 things that have, for some reason or another, captivated our attention. Each time we share a new set with you, the old will go somewhere far away…maybe never to be seen again. Maybe. Anyway, here is the first set of not interesting things we wanted to share with you. Welcome to the club!
